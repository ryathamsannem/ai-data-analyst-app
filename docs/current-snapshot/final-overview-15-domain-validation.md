## Default scatter status

All 15 domains: **no default Overview scatter** when ≥4 non-scatter business charts exist. Explicit correlation/scatter via AI question routing is unchanged (out of scope for this pass).

## AI summary sanity (deterministic, no LLM)

Per file, backend checks:

- KPI cards and chart titles exist as summary **candidates**
- No wrong domain label leak (e.g. HR label on revenue datasets)
- Domain-relevant metric keywords appear in card/chart titles
- Primary mapped metric reflected in candidate corpus
- Not generic-only placeholder titles

**Result:** 15/15 PASS
